Meet Your Instructor

Chris Loggins

Designer & Pinegrow Evangelist

Who Is This Chris Guy?

I'm glad you asked! I'm a self-taught designer from the United States. My journey in learning to create for the web began at age 8 in 2008. I had my first client by the time I was 12. Then something amazing happened, in 2014 I came across a Medium article in which a man named Matjaz was being interviewed about his amazing new product, Pinegrow Web Editor. After reading that article, I became a Pinegrow user.

Why are You Doing This?

Over the years, I have been priviledged to work on projects of all sorts both big and small. I have been able to contribute a wide variety of skills to each. Some of those skills have been gained is thanks to Pinegrow. I learned how to develop HTML/CSS and WordPress themes just from playing/ working in the app, skills that have put me ahead of people who I would've never been considered over otherwise. It is as I was taught as a young kid, to whom much is given, much is expected. That means if you are blessed with something of value such as a skill, you are expected to benefit others. That is why I am doing this, to give back to the community which has given me so much. I'm not claiming to be a designer and a full-stack developer, but I can teach you, a Designer, how Pinegrow can change your workflow and your life. It's my pleasure to do so.
